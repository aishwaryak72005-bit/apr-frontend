Characters Information

Tanjiro Kamado

Tanjiro Kamado is a kind-hearted and hardworking boy who lives in the mountains with his family. One day, his family is attacked by demons, and only his sister Nezuko survives. However, she is turned into a demon.

After this tragedy, Tanjiro decides to become a Demon Slayer to protect people and find a cure for Nezuko. He trains very hard and joins the Demon Slayer Corps.

Tanjiro trains very hard to become stronger and protect people from demons. During his journey, he faces many powerful enemies and learns new breathing techniques. Even after facing pain and difficulties, he never gives up and continues fighting to save his sister Nezuko and help others.

Famous Quote

“No matter how many people you may lose, you have no choice but to go on living.”

Nezuko Kamado

Nezuko Kamado is one of the main characters in Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba and the younger sister of Tanjiro Kamado. After her family is attacked by demons, Nezuko is transformed into a demon. Even after becoming a demon, she continues to protect humans and stays by Tanjiro’s side throughout their journey.

Nezuko possesses powerful demon abilities that make her very strong in battle. She has incredible physical strength, fast healing powers, and unique Blood Demon Art techniques. Her fighting skills help her protect Tanjiro and defeat dangerous demons during their journey.

Appearance Nezuko has long black hair with orange-colored tips and pink eyes. She wears a pink kimono and carries a bamboo muzzle in her mouth. Journey Nezuko travels with Tanjiro as he searches for a cure to turn her back into a human. Together, they face many dangerous demons and protect innocent people.

Famous Quote

“Humans are to be protected and saved.”
